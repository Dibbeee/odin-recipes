Pasta Carbonara

Description

A classic, creamy carbonara with parmesan cheese, arugula
and the finest black pepper - Tellicherry! Just as good on a
Friday evening with a good glass of wine as for a luxury
lunch on the weekend.

Ingrediens

  • 500 grams of fresh spaghetti
  • 2 packages of bacon (cut into small 2cm peices)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 dl whipped cream
  • 2 dl grated parmesan
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 2 spice measures freshly ground Tellicherry black pepper
  • 70 grams of arugula
  • 4 egg yolks

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the instructions on the
    back of the package.
  2. Fry the bacon pieces crispy in a frying pan
  3. Whisk together eggs, cream, Parmesan cheese, salt and
    black pepper in a bowl
  4. Drain the pasta and pour it back into the pan, fold in the
    egg mixture and the fried bacon. Stir over low heat to a
    creamy paste.
For serving
  • Put it on a plate together with the arugula salad. Serve
    with an extra egg yolk and add a few extra touches with
    the pepper mill on top.
